// OPERATING PRINCIPLES 04 axioms
I

Systems over goals

Goals are checkpoints. Systems are the engine. Build the machine, not just the destination.

II

Structure over motivation

Motivation is volatile. Structure is reliable. Design your environment before you design your goals.

III

Iteration over emotion

Feelings are data, not directives. Ship, observe, revise. Repeat with precision.

IV

Output as proof of thought

Ideas without execution are fiction. What you build is the only honest statement you can make.
